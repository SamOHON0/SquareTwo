Starting a business in Ireland takes real courage. You've thought it through, taken the risk, filed the paperwork, and made it official. That's the hard part — and you've already done it.

Square Two exists for exactly this moment. We're a small, Irish-owned team that works with newly incorporated businesses right across the country — from Donegal to Cork, Dublin to Galway. We don't care whether you're a sole trader or a limited company, whether you have a clear vision or are still figuring it out.

What we care about is getting your business visible online, quickly and professionally, so you can start doing what you actually incorporated to do. No jargon, no lengthy back-and-forth, no waiting weeks for something that should take days.
